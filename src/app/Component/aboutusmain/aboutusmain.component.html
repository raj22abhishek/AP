ABOUT US

Salt Pharmaceuticals is a leading global pharmaceutical marketing company, headquartered in Himachal Pradesh. We offer multiple therapeutic categories such as Analgesic, Antibiotics, Antihistamine, Antipyretic, Antiseptic, Antiemetic, Antacids, Antifungal, Cardiac, Diabetic, Iron & Calcium Supplements, Multivitamin Supplements and many more. All products are manufactured in facilities that are approved by major global regulatory agencies such as WHO GMP, UK MHRA, ANVISA Brazil and many more We are committed to delivering the best and most reliable medicines to our customers in India as well as abroad as we are one of the leading pharmaceutical export companies in India. We provide quality products to the clients all over the world. We have established a wide marketing network that is operative in domestic as well as in international market. We export to over 20 countries across the globe with more than 60% of its revenues derived from international operations. Adhering to its brand promise of being dedicated to life in all its dimensions.
